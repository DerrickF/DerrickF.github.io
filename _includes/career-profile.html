Career Profile

My name is Derrick Fink and I am a T-Shaped developer currently living and working in beautiful Boise Idaho. Wait, what the heck is a “T-Shaped” developer? Well first let me say that T-Shaped != Full Stack Developer I used to think of myself as a full stack developer until I came across this great article The Myth of the Full Stack Developer A T-Shaped developer is someone who has a particular specialty in one area but is comfortable doing a wide range of development work in other areas a well. My particular expertise is in back end .Net C# using things like Entity Framework and Web API. I also am comfortable in areas like, client side development with AngualrJS, and Dev Ops using AWS, TeamCity, and Octopus Deploy.
